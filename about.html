Peter A. O'Connor is a playwright and short story writer based in Perth, Western Australia.

His work blends dark humour with literary fiction and often explores regret, redemption, and the strange moments where the absurd and the tragic meet.

He writes stories and plays that move between the comic and the melancholy, with a particular interest in voices on the edge of change.
